PHASE 2 — Metaheuristic Optimization Layer
This sits on top of the trained AI models. The AI predicts the operating state (V, θ), and the optimizer finds optimal generator dispatch.
Tri-objective problem:

Minimize: Carbon emissions (f₁)
Minimize: Active power losses (f₂)
Minimize: Voltage deviation from nominal (f₃)

Recommended metaheuristic algorithms (compare them):
AlgorithmReasonNSGA-IIIMulti-objective, Pareto front generationMOPSOFast, good for continuous dispatch variablesGrey Wolf Optimizer (GWO)Strong exploitation, works well on power systemsWhale Optimization Algorithm (WOA)Recent, strong benchmark results in OPFSHADE / L-SHADEDifferential evolution variant, very competitive
The optimizer takes AI-predicted V and θ as warm-start state estimates and optimizes generator set points (P_g) subject to:

Power balance constraints
Generator capacity limits
Voltage magnitude limits [0.95, 1.05] p.u.
Line thermal limits
