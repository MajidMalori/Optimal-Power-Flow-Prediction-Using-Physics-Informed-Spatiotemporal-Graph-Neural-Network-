Efficiency and convergence metrics in computational performance aim to evaluate both solution time and solution convergence aspects of OPF learning models. The computational efficiency metrics offer valuable insights into the efficiency and speed of model execution, particularly when compared to computationally expensive approaches, addressing a significant challenge within this domain.
